About Me

My name is Doris Afriyie Amoakohene and I am an Msc. Informatics student at the The School of Informatics, Computing, and Cyber Systems (SICCS) of Northern Arizona University. I am honored to be advised by Toby Hocking/a> As part of my Masters, I work as a contributor to the data.table packages in R. I am broadly interested in data anlytics, Computing and Open source

I earned my Bachelor's degree in Statistics from Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology-Kumasi, Ghana (KNUST). I worked with the National Communication Authority, the body that regulate Mobile Networks in ghana as a billing and analysis officer and later moved to Deloitte to work with Financial Advisory as a Corporate Finance Advisor, I moved to Northern Arizona University (NAU) for my Master's degree in Informatics.
