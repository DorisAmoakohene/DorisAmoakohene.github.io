About Me

Doris Afriyie Amoakohene is a Master's student in Informatics at the The School of Informatics, Computing, and Cyber Systems (SICCS) of Northern Arizona University. where she is advised by Toby Hocking/a > As part of her graduate studies, she contributes to the development of the data.table package in R. Her research interests include data analytics, computing, and open-source technologies.

Doris holds a Bachelor's degree in Statistics from Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology-Kumasi, Ghana (KNUST). Prior to pursuing her graduate degree, she worked as a Billing and Analysis Officer at the National Communication Authority in Ghana and later as a Corporate Finance Advisor in the Financial Advisory department at Deloitte. In 2023 she movet to Northern Arizona University (NAU) for her Master's degree in Informatics.
